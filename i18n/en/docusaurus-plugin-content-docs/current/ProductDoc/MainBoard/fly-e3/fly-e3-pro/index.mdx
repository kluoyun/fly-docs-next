## Product Specifications

    * 32-bit ARM Cortex-M4 series 168 MHz, STM32F407ZGT6 chip
    * Firmware: RepRap/Klipper
    * Onboard five TMC2209 drivers
    * Display: Serial touchscreen, mini12864 LCD, 12864 LCD, 2004 LCD, FLY 4.3/7.0 V1
    * 1 high-voltage IN port, supports auto bed leveling sensors: BLTouch, PL08N, etc., Klicky, Voron Tap, etc.
    * Using RepRap firmware, it can use an ESP32 WiFi module, supporting up to 1.6m/s
    * Unique PWM Fan MOS board design, easily replaceable, reducing customer repair difficulty, four 2-pin fan connectors
    * PCB uses 2OZ copper thickness, capable of handling higher current
    * 2 ADC interfaces, supporting up to two thermistors
    * 4 IO interfaces, can be used for leveling and limit switches
    * 3 heater interfaces, supporting up to three heaters





:::tip
Open your browser to access FLY-Tools at your IP:9999
:::

    * To enable the screen with TFT, you need to turn on the following options
    * Please note that there are three types of TFT screens:
        * `FLY-TFT-V1` V1 resistive screen, suitable for all FLY hosts
        * `FLY-TFT-V2-R` V2 resistive screen, suitable for C8 and PI-V2 and Raspberry Pi series with 40-pin, but requires G2T to be used together
        * `FLY-TFT-V2-C` V2 capacitive screen, suitable for C8 and PI-V2 and Raspberry Pi series with 40-pin, but requires G2T to be used together

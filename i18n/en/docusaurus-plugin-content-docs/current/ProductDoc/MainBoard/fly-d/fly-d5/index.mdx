# Introduction

> Mellow Fly-D5 is a high-performance 3D printer motherboard launched by the Fly3D mainboard R&D team of Guangzhou Millun Electronic Technology Co., Ltd, supporting up to 5 stepper motors.

<ImageView image={require('@site/docs/ProductDoc/MainBoard/fly-d/fly-d5/img/d5.webp').default} size="60%" align="center" />

## **1. Product Features**

* Equipped with a 32-bit ARM Cortex-M0+ series STM32F072RBT6 main control chip running at 48MHz
* Supports CAN bus connection, providing more stable data transmission, lower latency, and more stable connections
* Driver support: A4988, LV8729, DRV8225, TMC2208/2209/5160, etc.
* Onboard DIAG function pins, allowing for easy use of endstop support through simple jumper cap insertion/removal

## 2. Product Specifications

* MCU: STM32F072RBT6
* Firmware: Klipper/Marlin
* Power Supply: 12-24V DC, providing more stable power supply and eliminating the need for a voltage drop module
* Driver Operation Modes: SPI, UART, STEP/DIR
* Supported Drivers: External TMC5160Pro, TMC5160, TMC2209, TMC2225, TMC2226, TMC2208, TMC2130, A4988, etc.
* Expansion Interfaces: BLTouch (Servos, Probe), UART X1, 12864 X1
* Fans: Two controllable fans X2, Parallel fans X2, Always-on fans X2
* Temperature Sensors: Two NTC 100K channels
* Heating Interfaces: Two controllable interfaces supporting up to 10A
* Supported Machine Structures: Cartesian, Delta, Kossel, Ultimaker, CoreXY

## Preparations Before System Burn-in

    * Card reader
    * A TF card with a capacity of no less than 16GB or no more than 128GB, and the transmission speed must be Class10 or higher
    * Download the corresponding <Button variant="contained" disableElevation href="/docs/ResDownload/system-img/fly-lite2">system image</Button>
    * Download the required <Button variant="contained" disableElevation href="/docs/ResDownload/auxiliary_software/">burning tool</Button>

## System Burn-in

:::info
* Lite 2 can only burn the system to a TF card and cannot use M2WE
* After the system burn-in is completed, the TF card needs to be installed on Lite 2

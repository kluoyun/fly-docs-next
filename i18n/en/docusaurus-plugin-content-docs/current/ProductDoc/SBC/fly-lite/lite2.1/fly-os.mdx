## Preparations Before System Flashing

    * Card Reader
    * One TF card with a capacity of no less than 16GB or no more than 128GB, and the transmission speed of the TF card must be Class10 or higher
    * Download the corresponding <Button variant="contained" disableElevation href="/docs/ResDownload/system-img/fly-lite2">System Image</Button>
    * Download the required <Button variant="contained" disableElevation href="/docs/ResDownload/auxiliary_software/">Burning Tool</Button>

## System Flashing

:::info
* Lite 2.1 can only flash the system to the TF card and cannot use M2WE
* After the system flashing is completed, the TF card needs to be installed on Lite 2.1
:::

Mainboard

Precisely control your 3D printer core, supporting Klipper, RRF, Marlin and other firmwares to provide excellent stability and compatibility, ensuring every print is flawless.

Host computer

As the brain of the 3D printer, the host computer provides powerful data processing capabilities and control functions to ensure efficient, high-speed and accurate printing experience.

Tool heads

Highly compatible tool head board specially adapted for various printer types, easily connect to CANBus, RS232, USB and other communication protocols to achieve diversified printing.

Display

High-definition touch screen interface, intuitive operation, real-time monitoring of printing status, and wireless multi-control group control put each printer under control.

Expansion Boards & Modules

Enhance your 3D printer’s capabilities and easily expand with more sensors and accessories for endless possibilities.

drive

A wide range of professional drives to optimize printer performance, print accuracy and speed to provide the best support for your projects.

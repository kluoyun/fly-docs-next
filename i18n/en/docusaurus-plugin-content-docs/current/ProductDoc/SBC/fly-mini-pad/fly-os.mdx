## Preparations Before System Burning

    * Card Reader
    * One TF card with a capacity of no less than 16GB or no more than 128GB. The TF card's transfer speed must be Class10 or higher.
    * Download the corresponding <Button variant="contained" disableElevation href="/docs/ResDownload/system-img/fly-minipad">system image</Button>
    * Download the required <Button variant="contained" disableElevation href="/docs/ResDownload/auxiliary_software/">burning tool</Button>

## System Burning

:::info
* MINI PAD can only burn the system to the TF card and cannot use M2WE.
* After burning the system, you need to install the TF card into the MINI PAD.
:::

## Main Board Wiring

:::warning Note
Due to the high resolution of the images, it cannot be clearly viewed when browsing on the web page. Please place the mouse cursor over the wiring diagram, right-click, and save the image to the desktop to view!
:::

:::warning Warning
Do not use the purchased finished **three-wire limit switch**, as this type of limit switch may cause the **main board short circuit** and **damage the main board**!!! Please connect the wires strictly according to the wiring method shown in the wiring diagram!!!
:::
